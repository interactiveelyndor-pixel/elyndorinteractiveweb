Kaali: A Mother's Tale

A harrowing journey through grief, vengeance, and redemption. Experience a narrative-driven action adventure set in ancient India.

Episode 1: Echoes of the Past Cinematic Trailer

Brief Lore

Anika's world is shattered the moment her son disappears without a trace. Driven by a mother's frantic desperation, she tracks him to the isolating fog of an abandoned medical facility. But this place is not empty. Behind the rusted doors and blood-stained wards lies a sinister history where human cruelty meddled with things meant to stay buried.

Now, trapped in a decaying nightmare, Anika must face an unspeakable, ancient terror if she ever hopes to bring her son home.
